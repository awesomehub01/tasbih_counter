Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

**Tasbih Counter & Reminders** ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

# Information We Collect

## 1. Account Information

If you choose to sign in using your Google account, we may collect:

* Your name
* Your email address
* Your Google profile photo

This information is provided by Google Authentication and is used only to personalize your experience and synchronize your data across devices.

---

## 2. User Content

The app stores information that you create, including:

* Favorite Tasbih
* Reminder schedules
* Reminder settings
* Feedback or messages submitted through the Contact Us feature

This information is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore and may also be stored locally on your device to improve performance and offline access.

---

## 3. Local Device Storage

To provide offline functionality, the app stores certain information locally on your device, including:

* Reading history (if enabled)
* Favorite Tasbih
* Reminder information
* App settings
* Selected Tasbih
* Theme preferences

Local data remains on your device unless removed by you or by uninstalling the application.

---

## 4. Notifications

If you create reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device.

These notifications are generated locally and are used solely to remind you to perform your selected Tasbih or Dhikr.

---

## 5. Analytics and Crash Information

The current version of the app does not intentionally collect analytics or crash reports.

If analytics or crash reporting services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
